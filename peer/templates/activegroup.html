The weblink on the active projects page is composed of the website-url of the form http://server/website and the long name. You must use a complete url, that is,
including the http:// part, otherwise the visiting browsers think it is a page on this server, relative to peerweb. And that, dear people, is quite unlikely.

Also remember to use a proper youtube link like http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=<someCode> because from this link we derive the intro picture on our overview pages such as http://mini.fontysvenlo.org.
