How to enter critique

If the author was smart he added page and even linenumbers to the document, so you can use those as coordinates to those spots in the documents you want to critisize.

You may use html constructs (like lists with <ol> etc), which can be usefull to get your point across. You can even link to external documents with an ordinary <a href=...> construct.
