Boe
The Fontys Technische Hogeschool Venlo peerweb allows students of the Fontys Campus Venlo to
\begin{itemize}
\item Peer assess each other. Students in project teams grade each other on various aspects 
  to the effect that the inter group process is also taken into account when giving individual 
  grades for the project results. The aspects are setup by the project tutor.
\item Upload project results in the form of files. On upload, project team members and 
  the tutor are automatically warned per email.
\item Critisize or give each other feedback on those results. This feedback is considered 
  important for the grade of both the author of the document and the critisizer.
\end{itemize}

to log in into the peer assessment web site.
